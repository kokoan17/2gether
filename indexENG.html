About Us

2gether is a service that helps you find new
contacts at university. Everything is implemented
directly using a telegram bot that takes into
account the interests of students.

With 2gether you will find guys with similar
interests, be with us, be in communication.

Relevance

We have identified 3 types of students

Resident of Moscow

Lives in Moscow all his life, has friends,
feels quite comfortable, has enough communication

As a rule, he does not need new communication so much.

Foreigner

All my life I lived in another country, let alone in another city,
the likelihood of having friends in Moscow is very small,
feels less comfortable, most likely there is not enough communication

As a rule, there is a very strong need for communication.

Nonresident of Moscow

I’ve lived in Omsk all my life (conditional example), I don’t have many good friends in Moscow
, and maybe even none, but I understand the people here,
getting a connection is not the biggest problem,
I don’t feel so comfortable , but still.

As a rule, needs new communication.
